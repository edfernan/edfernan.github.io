My Doctor Online: Cold and Flu

Participatory Design Sessions

The "Cold and Flu" section of My Doctor Online is intended to help patients get a flu vaccination and guide patients on the best way to care for their cold/flu symptoms. This research project helped gather an understanding of how patients utilize Kaiser Permanente resources and provide strategies to reduce the number of patients who seek medical care for symptoms that do not require clinical intervention.

User Testing | Secondary Research

The research project for the diabetes section of My Doctor Online was intended to identify usability issues. However, we ran into recruiting challenges that ended up providing valuable insight into our Spanish-speaking patient base.

Summer Search Alumni Board

Design-thinking Workshop

Summer Search is a national college success organization that works to close the opportunity gap for high potential low-income students by helping transform what they believe is possible. As chair of the Alumni Board, I co-led a workshop to define our goals and develop approaches to engage our Alumni community.
