About Me

As a native of the Napa Valley, people always asked if my family owned a winery. They didn't and I likely won't either in my lifetime. I did however spend part of my childhood living on a vineyard taking my mom's cooking to my dad during lunch hour on a little blue bicycle. Being the son of immigrants provided me with experiences that were personally ordinary yet provided me a perspective to view problems from a different lense. With hard work and supportive people who invested in me along the way, I now work in user experience and service design trying to unravel complex problems to build a better tomorrow.

In my 5 years as a UX professional, I've been fortunate to help Spanish-speaking patients navigate health challenges, help job-seekers apply for new career opportunities, and give back to community organizations.
